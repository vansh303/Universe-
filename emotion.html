Emotions Speak Louder

Tujhse jyada jo lage khubsurat mujhe, aisa to koi heera nhi bana ab tak iss jamane me,
Hum jo puri aankhein khol kar bhi nhi dekh pate hai ye chand ka tukda kabhi,
Iss baat se to lagta hai aayina bhi toot jata hoga usse ke jara se muskura se,
Aur tere chehre se jhulfon ko hatane per aisa lagta hai mujhe maano gehre samundar me bhi tujhe dekh kar aag lag jaati hogi abhi,
Taaj banane walo se bhi kayi jyada kimti honge haath usseke jissne itna khubsurat ajuba banakar diya hoga iss janame ko...
